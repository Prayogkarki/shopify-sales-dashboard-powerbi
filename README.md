# 🛍️ Shopify Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Shopify Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze e-commerce performance across regions, customers, and products.

The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and payment preferences, enabling data-driven decision-making for business growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze sales performance across regions and cities  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Track sales trends over time  
- Evaluate payment method preferences  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization  
- Excel/CSV – Dataset  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- 💰 Net Sales  
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold  
- 👥 Total Customers  
- 🔁 Repeat Customers  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 1. Regional Analysis

#### 🌍 Province-Level Analysis
- Filled Map to visualize sales distribution across provinces  
- Color intensity represents selected KPI (Sales, Customers, Quantity)  

#### 📍 City-Level Analysis
- Bubble/Density Map to show city-wise performance  
- Bubble size represents selected metric  

#### 📊 City Performance Comparison
- Bar chart showing top-performing cities  
- Dynamically updates based on selected KPI  

---

### 🔹 2. Sales Trend Analysis

#### 📅 Daily Trend
- Area chart showing daily sales or customer trends  

#### ⏰ Hourly Trend
- Bar/Line chart displaying activity by hour (0–23)  
- Identifies peak shopping hours  

---

### 🔹 3. Payment Method Analysis
- Identify most and least used payment gateways  
- Understand customer payment preferences  
- Helps optimize checkout experience  

---

### 🔹 4. Product Analysis
- Sales by product type/category  
- Identify high-performing products  
- Analyze customer engagement across categories  

---

### 🔹 5. Detailed Drill-Through
- Transaction-level data view  
- Drill-through from summary visuals  
- Explore individual orders, customers, and products  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Certain provinces and cities generate the majority of sales  
- Peak sales occur during specific hours of the day  
- Repeat customers contribute significantly to total revenue  
- A few product categories dominate overall sales performance  
- Payment preferences vary across regions and customers
